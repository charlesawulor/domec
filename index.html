About The Founder

Mr. Giovanni is originally from Rome, Italy. Mr. Giovanni took after his father who’s also a Marine Engineer. He is intimately familiar with the local environment and has spent a great deal of his life boating and fishing in the area. He earned his Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in marine and civil engineering from the University of Florida and has been practicing in Florida since. Mr. Giovanni has served as principal engineer on numerous multi-million-dollar oil rig projects in Florida, Texas, Scotland, UK and the Caribbean. He is a recognized expert in civil, coastal and marine construction and is frequently asked to serve as expert witness. Mr. Giovanni is a registered Professional Marine Engineer in the State of Florida and Texas.
